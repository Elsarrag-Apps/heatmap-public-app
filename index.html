Urban Heat Risk Dashboard

This interactive dashboard enables users to explore climate-related urban heat risk across UK regions. The map allows postcode-level insights into overheating vulnerability in buildings under different climate scenarios.

Users can examine location-specific overheating projections, building performance risk levels, and see the effect of passive and active mitigation strategies over time.

This tool is ideal for policy analysts, urban planners, and built environment professionals addressing climate adaptation and urban resilience.
